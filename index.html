Exploring Feelings – Happy Bunnies Nursery

In our Babyroom, we are exploring textures by offering the children fabric boxes, coloured playdough and ice play. We will be having ice cubes with animals hidden inside.

Visit Us

In Jungle room, we are exploring some action and finger rhymes. the children will get to explore playdough and speak about big and small and seeing how shapes change such as round ball to flat.

The children will also listen to some everyday sounds and see what they can identify and if they can name them

In our preschool room, the children are re visiting jubilee and speaking about their own experiences. we will be continuing to practice for our graduation as it’s fast approaching. The children will also explore feelings using the feeling cushions to help identify how they feel and also how others feel.
